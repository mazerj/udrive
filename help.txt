Keypad
---------------------

Escape	abort current movement NOW!

up	small step up (out of brain) -- 1 jog
down	small step down (into brain) -- 1 jog

pageUp	big step up (out of brain) -- 10 jogs
pageDn	big step down (out of brain) -- 10 jogs

space/n	next motor
p	previous motor
g	select/goto motor <arg>

aA	toggle 'move all' flag
lL	lock current drive
qQ	quit


s	set current motor step size to <arg> steps
S	set all motor step sizes to <arg> steps
u	set current motor step size to <arg> um
U	set all motor step sizes to <arg> um

0123456789 + BackSpace: enter/edit numeric argument

Joypad
---------------------

   Y
  X B
   Z

  Y = up
  A = down
  X = prev motor
  B = next motor
  R-trig = abort
